Building Reliable Power Infrastructure

Since 1990 Founded in 1990 by Mr. V. Thimma Raju, Janani Electricals Private Limited is a Government of Karnataka licensed super grade electrical contractor specializing in comprehensive electrical installation. From modest beginnings, the company has grown into a trusted industry leader serving residential, commercial, industrial, hospitality, and infrastructure sectors. Through consistent dedication to quality and innovation, Janani Electricals Private Limited has earned recognition for delivering reliable electrical solutions with uncompromising standards.

Our Founder

V. Thimma Raju With over four decades of experience in the electrical industry, Mr. V. Thimma Raju established Janani Electricals Private Limited with a vision to provide dependable, high-quality electrical contracting services. His leadership philosophy is centered on integrity, professionalism, technical excellence, and customer satisfaction. Under his guidance, the company has successfully executed numerous landmark projects and continues to set benchmarks in electrical contracting.
